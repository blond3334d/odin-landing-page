Beyond the lens, Into the Enchantment
ma·ra·hu·yo (verb) : to be enchanted; to be led into a state of wonder or attraction.
Inspired by the ancient Filipino word for 'enchantment,' CineMarahuyo curates a selection of films that do more than entertain — they enchant.
Four Ways to Lose Yourself in the Magic
IMAX — Step into a world beyond the screen with towering visuals and breathtaking, immersive sound.
4DX — Feel every pulse-pounding moment with motion-controlled seating and sensory effects that bring action life.
Drive-In — Experience the timeless magic of cinema under the sky and the stars from the comfort of your car.
Standard — Enjoy the pure, classic movie-going experience with crystal-clear projection and perfect, high-fidelity digital audio.
